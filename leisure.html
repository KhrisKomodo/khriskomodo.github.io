My Hobbies

This page is what I do in my free time! While it is titled "hobbies"

Speedcubing

I first learnt to solve a Rubik's Cube in my Senior Year of high school. I used online resources and a mix of my own intuition, and overtime I only got faster. Since then, I have learnt to solve a variety of other twisty puzzles and even attended the first ever official World Cube Association (WCA) competition in my home country of Belize.

I currently practice and participate in a number of events:

  • 2x2
  • 3x3
  • 3x3 One-Handed (OH)
  • 4x4
  • 5x5
  • 6x6
  • 7x7
  • Megaminx

I have previously competed in speedsolving the Pyraminx, but haven't practiced in months. I plan to eventually learn and compete in the various Blindfolded (BLD) events hosted by the WCA, which are 3BLD, 4BLD, 5BLD, and Multi-BLD.

Music

I have always been musically inclined. When I was 14, I finally got my first guitar and began learning. Since then, I have picked up the bass guitar as well, and my knowledge of music theory has allowed me to compose parts for instruments I don't have the physical technique for yet. I have performed a handfull of times; once as a guitarist for my high school's choir as a Senior, once spontaneously joining two performers at a dinner event I was at, and several times at private family events.

In addition to playing, I listen to and enjoy music from a variety of genres, such as:

  • Rock
  • Metal
  • Hip-Hop
  • Soca
  • Reggae
  • K-pop
  • J-pop
  • J-rock
  • And many more!

Regarding specific bands and artists, I find myself regularly listening to:

  • Kes
